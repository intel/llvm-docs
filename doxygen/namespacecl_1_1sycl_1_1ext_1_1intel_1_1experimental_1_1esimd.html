enum cl::sycl::ext::intel::experimental::esimd::EsimdFenceMask

Bits used to form the bitmask that controls the behavior of esimd_fence Bit 0: the “commit enable” bit.

If set, the fence is guaranteed to be globally observable Bit 1: flush instruction cache if set.
