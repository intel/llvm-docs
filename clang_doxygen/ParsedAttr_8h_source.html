231 /// ParsedAttr - Represents a syntactic attribute.

234 ///
235 /// 1: __attribute__(( const )). ParmName/Args/NumArgs will all be unused.
236 /// 2: __attribute__(( mode(byte) )). ParmName used, Args/NumArgs unused.
