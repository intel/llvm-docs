Detailed Description

This represents the 'align' clause in the '#pragma omp allocate' directive.

#pragma omp allocate(a) allocator(omp_default_mem_alloc) align(8)

In this example directive '#pragma omp allocate' has simple 'allocator' clause with the allocator 'omp_default_mem_alloc' and align clause with value of 8.
