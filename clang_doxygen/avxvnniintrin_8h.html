static

Multiply groups of 4 adjacent pairs of unsigned 8-bit integers in __A with corresponding signed 8-bit integers in __B, producing 4 intermediate signed 16-bit results.

Sum these 4 results with the corresponding 32-bit integer in __S, and store the packed 32-bit results in DST.

This intrinsic corresponds to the VPDPBUSD instructions.

\operation FOR j := 0 to 7 tmp1.word := Signed(ZeroExtend16(__A.byte[4*j]) * SignExtend16(__B.byte[4*j])) tmp2.word := Signed(ZeroExtend16(__A.byte[4*j+1]) * SignExtend16(__B.byte[4*j+1])) tmp3.word := Signed(ZeroExtend16(__A.byte[4*j+2]) * SignExtend16(__B.byte[4*j+2])) tmp4.word := Signed(ZeroExtend16(__A.byte[4*j+3]) * SignExtend16(__B.byte[4*j+3])) DST.dword[j] := __S.dword[j] + tmp1 + tmp2 + tmp3 + tmp4 ENDFOR DST[MAX:256] := 0 \endoperation
